🎵 A版 · Not Lost

莎总 / Lysa

🎛️ 莎总感觉的风格Prompt

[track: genre: hip-hop, melodic rap, conscious rap, style: cinematic, warm, gospel-influenced, mood: bittersweet hopeful, length: 240]
[male: emotional, raw, melodic rap delivery]
[instruments: piano, synth bass, 808 drums, gospel choir textures, ambient pad]
[tempo: mid-tempo hip-hop groove]
[background-vocals: harmonic, layered, gospel choir]

方向：方向A·微光励志 — 没有一句"加油"，所有力量感都来自"被爱"本身。Melodic Rap叙事流，Hook用大开口元音/oʊ/（home/alone/whole/soul），福音感和声层叠。Bridge从说唱转chant爆发"I'm not lost / I'm just loved"，私密忏悔室感→Final Chorus大合唱，对比最强。

Intro · Spoken Word
[intro | instruments: solo piano, ambient pad, spoken word]
Yeah... this one's for the ones who never let us fall
The voice in the static, the light through the wall
You know who you are.
Verse 1 · Rap
[verse | style: narrative, introspective, vocals: emotional rap delivery]
They told me toughen up, don't let 'em see you bleed
Put a mask on, boy, that's what they need
But late nights when the city sleep, I stare at the ceiling
Wondering if any of this chasing even got a meaning
Phone buzzing, notifications screaming my name
But none of 'em asking if I'm okay, it's all the same
Pressure building like a storm inside my chest
Feeling like a puzzle with a piece that won't connect
Then I hear your voice, not through the phone, through the years
Saying "run your race, I'll be right here"
And suddenly the weight don't feel so heavy no more
'Cause I know there's a harbor waiting on the shore
Pre-Chorus · Melodic
[pre-chorus | style: building tension, vocals: melodic transition]
When the ground starts shaking and the walls cave in
When I'm standing at the edge wondering where to begin
I remember...
Chorus · Hook (Gospel Feel)
[chorus | style: anthemic, vocals: powerful emotional belt, instruments: full drums + choir swell]
Everybody needs someone to call home
A hand that won't let go when you're out there all alone
You taught me broken pieces still can make a whole
Now I walk with heavy gold, 'cause your love is my soul
[background-vocals: layered, gospel choir ad-libs]
Not lost, not lost
Just loved, just loved
Verse 2 · Rap
[verse-2 | style: narrative deepening, vocals: emotional rap delivery]
Used to think I had to carry it all by myself
Like asking for help was putting pride up on the shelf
But you showed me strength ain't about never breaking
It's about knowing who to call when your hands start shaking
Every scar I got, you saw it before I did
Every time I fell, you were praying where I hid
And I ain't religious but I feel something holy
When you look at me like I'm somebody worth knowing
The world wants me harder, colder, meaner, numb
But your voice in my head says "just come home, son"
So I keep my head up, not 'cause I'm fearless
But 'cause I know behind me, your love is the clearest
Bridge · Build-up & Burst
[bridge | style: intense, dynamic, vocals: raw emotional burst, instruments: stripped + rebuild]
I was shattered, scattered on the floor
You walked in, didn't ask what for
Just started picking up the parts of me
Saying "you're not lost, you're just finding where to be"
And I cried, yeah I broke down in your arms
No performance, no alarms
Just a kid who needed somebody to see
The mess underneath and still believe in me
I'm not lost, I'm not lost
I'm just loved, I'm just loved
At whatever cost
Final Chorus · Full Choir
[final-chorus | style: full anthemic, vocals: soaring + choir, instruments: full orchestra + drums]
[background-vocals: full gospel choir, layered harmonies, ad-libs]
Everybody needs someone to call home
A hand that won't let go when you're out there all alone
You taught me broken pieces still can make a whole
Now I walk with heavy gold, 'cause your love is my soul
Not lost, not lost
Just loved, just loved
Outro · Spoken Fade
[outro | style: fading, vocals: soft spoken, instruments: piano + vocal echo]
Yeah... they say it takes somebody to love somebody
I feel that now
For everyone who ever held us up...
This is yours.
(fade out with piano + vocal echo)

🎵 B版 · Quiet Strength

GPT-4o-mini

🎛️ GPT版风格Prompt

[track: genre: hip-hop, pop rap, style: uplifting, warm, cinematic, mood: hopeful inspiring, length: 240]
[male: clear, strong, emotional rap delivery]
[instruments: piano, acoustic guitar, live drums, strings, synth pad]
[tempo: mid-tempo, steady hip-hop groove]
[background-vocals: harmonic, layered, ethereal]

方向：偏传统励志说唱，韵脚规整（AABB/CDEE），旋律温暖。"rise above""treasure of gold"等正能量词较多，微光感被稀释。适合主流励志短视频，但方向A的"被爱=不迷路"细腻感稍弱。Hook概念好（quiet strength），但旋律展开空间因韵脚变化多而受限。

Intro
[intro | instruments: soft piano, ambient pad, spoken word]
In the shadows where the whispers dwell,
There's a light that flickers, but it shines so well.
Sometimes it's the silence that speaks the loudest.
Verse 1 · Rap
[verse | style: reflective, storytelling, vocals: emotional rap]
I tread through valleys where the shadows loom,
Doubt's a heavy weight, it fills the room.
Lost in the noise, where dreams fade fast,
But I keep my heart close, gotta outlast.
Walls built high, but I'm learning to climb,
Every stumble and fall, I'm still in my prime.
Got a crew that's tight, like stitches on skin,
They remind me I'm golden, even when I feel thin.
Life's a puzzle, pieces scattered in the dark,
But every broken shard ignites a spark.
Pre-Chorus
[pre-chorus | style: melodic lift, building energy, vocals: smooth transition]
In the silence, I breathe, I find my way,
Strength in the stillness, I'm here to stay.
Whispers of love like a soft embrace,
Together we rise, in this sacred space.
Chorus · Hook
[chorus | style: anthemic, vocals: powerful + layered harmonies, instruments: full drums + strings]
We are the light when the world feels dim,
Holding each other with love from within.
You're enough, oh, you're more than enough,
Through the storm and the struggle, we rise above.
Together we shine, our hearts intertwined,
In this quiet strength, true peace we find.
Verse 2 · Rap
[verse-2 | style: emotional deepening, vocals: raw but controlled rap]
Now I'm peeling back layers, showing my scars,
Vulnerability's strength, it's written in stars.
Fighting battles unseen, but feel the weight lift,
Love's a gentle force, it's the ultimate gift.
Every tear I shed, waters the ground,
From the roots of my soul, new life is found.
I'm not just a fighter, I'm learning to heal,
When the night feels heavy, I know it's real.
With each echoing heartbeat, I rise from the dust,
In this tapestry of life, it's love that I trust.
Bridge · Build-up
[bridge | style: intense chant-like, building, vocals: powerful group energy]
Here we are, together, in the fray,
Feel the pulse of love, guiding our way.
When the night is cold, we'll ignite the flame,
In this unity, we call each other's name.
Oh, we're more than the battles, we're stories untold,
This quiet strength, a treasure of gold.
Together we rise, let the world see,
In our harmony, we're truly free.
Final Chorus · Full Power
[final-chorus | style: full power, explosive, vocals: choir backing, instruments: full band]
[background-vocals: full choir, soaring harmonies]
We are the light when the world feels dim,
Holding each other with love from within.
You're enough, oh, you're more than enough,
Through the storm and the struggle, we rise above.
Together we shine, our hearts intertwined,
In this quiet strength, true peace we find.
Outro · Fade
[outro | style: gentle reflective fade, vocals: soft, instruments: piano fade]
So remember the whispers, let them guide your way,
You're never alone, in love we'll stay.
This quiet strength, forever our grace.

🎵 C版 · The Low Hum

Gemini-2.5-pro

🎛️ Gemini版风格Prompt

[track: genre: hip-hop, melodic rap, conscious rap, style: cinematic, warm, introspective, mood: bittersweet hopeful, length: 240]
[male: emotional, raw, J. Cole-style narrative delivery]
[instruments: piano, synth bass, 808 drums, ambient city textures, gospel choir textures]
[tempo: mid-tempo, slow-burning hip-hop groove]
[background-vocals: harmonic, layered, subtle gospel warmth]

方向：方向A契合度最强。J. Cole叙事流，"hired than inspired""cracks in the ceiling"金句级细节密度。"low hum"核心隐喻贯穿全曲——Intro synth note → 贯穿"low hum" → Outro回归synth note，教科书级闭环。Bridge "not about the winnin', it's about the beginnin'"精准捕捉微光感：不是赢，是还在。Verse 2 "You just held the space"心理咨询术语入词，极当代。

Intro · Spoken
[intro | instruments: solo piano, distant city ambience, low synth note, spoken word]
(Spoken, over faint distant city ambience and a single low synth note)
The world's so damn loud, you know?
Everyone's shouting what you're supposed to be.
Sometimes... you just need one quiet sound to hold onto.
Verse 1 · Rap
[verse | style: narrative, introspective, weary grind, vocals: rhythmic storytelling rap]
Yeah, another sunrise bleeds on the windowsill
Another climb up a cold and lonely hill
Got my jaw set tight, my reflection looks tired
Chasin' a dream that feels more hired than inspired
The concrete canyons echo with the promises I made
While the sirens sing the blues of a debt that's gettin' paid
Shoulders carryin' the weight of a skyline I ain't even build
Tryna fill a space that the world told me to get filled
They say "keep your head up," but my neck is sore as hell
Just one more crack before this whole damn castle fell.
Pre-Chorus · Melodic
[pre-chorus | style: melodic lift, clarity moment, vocals: sung transition]
But then the static breaks, for just a second I see through
And in the noise, I get a single thought of you
It ain't a lightning strike, just a flicker in the dark
A quiet, steady beat that re-ignites the spark.
Chorus · Hook (Gospel Choir)
[chorus | style: anthemic, warm gospel choir feel, vocals: soaring + layered harmonies, instruments: drums + piano + choir]
Oh-oh-oh, I don't need a king's crown
Just the low hum of your sound
To know I'm on solid ground
Oh-oh-oh, it brings me back home
Makes me know I'm not alone
Yeah, that's all I need to know.
Verse 2 · Rap
[verse-2 | style: emotional deepening, social media critique, vocals: raw introspective rap]
I see 'em postin' wins, the highlight reels and trophies
While I'm just countin' cracks in the ceiling, feelin' hopeless
An imposter in my own skin, wearin' last year's confidence
Tryna build a fortress out of common sense
They sell you dreams in a bottle, tell you "man up" and "be strong"
And you start to believe that showin' hurt is somethin' wrong
But you never did that, you never bought the paper lies
You just saw the fractures and the fear behind my eyes
You didn't offer answers or a five-step master plan
You just held the space and let me know you understand.
Bridge · Build-up & Burst
[bridge | style: stripped, chant-like build, vocals: raw vulnerability, instruments: minimal drums]
It's not about the winnin'
It's about the beginnin'
Of another day... and knowin' you're still in it.
It's not about the praise
It's just gettin' through the haze...
And your voice is the only thing that stays.
And that's enough...
Just knowing is enough.
Final Chorus · Full Power
[final-chorus | style: full power, explosive, choir vocals swell, instruments: full band + gospel choir]
[background-vocals: full gospel choir, ad-libs: "Yeah!", "That's all I need!"]
Oh-oh-oh, I don't need a king's crown!
Just the low hum of your sound!
To know I'm on solid ground!
Oh-oh-oh, it brings me back home!
Makes me know I'm not alone!
Yeah, that's all I need to know!
Outro · Spoken Fade
[outro | style: fading, returning to intro synth, vocals: soft spoken, instruments: low synth note fade]
(Spoken, as beat fades, leaving only the low synth note from intro)
The whole world can scream. It doesn't matter.
I can still hear it...
...that low hum.
